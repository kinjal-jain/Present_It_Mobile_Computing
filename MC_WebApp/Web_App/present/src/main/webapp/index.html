Present-It

Present-It is a complete solution system for your daily presentations where in you just upload your presentations with our system and find them accessible in your classroom as soon as you just walk in. Also you have the ability to control these presentation from your smartphone making this solution all the more interesting. This project is a part of the course work in the course Mobile Computing taught at University of Florida. We are doing it under the guidance of Dr. Sumi Helal. This project is sponsored by Google's 2016 Internet of Things (IoT) Technology Research Award Pilot.
